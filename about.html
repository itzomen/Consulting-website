Meet The CEO

Bochum Samuel Bache

    Bochum Samuel Bache, is a prolific, passionate and multiple award winning global peace ambassador, youth

    empowerment and community development leader, born in Banga, south west region of Cameroon.

    He lives and serves in Buea, Cameroon.He holds a Bsc. in Geography from the university of Buea Cameroon.
    He serves as the Cameroon National Youth Delegate to Commonwealth-UK under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and

    Civic Education,and the Commonwealth Department of the Ministry of External Relations, National Youth Delegate to African Youth Networks

    Summit in Pretoria-South Africa and Cameroon Youth Envoy for Peace and Democracy.

    He is the Founder/Executive Director of Youth Advocates for Peace and Community Empowerment Cameroon (YAPCEC).
    He has along many others volunteered with the Ministry of environment (MINEPDED), International center for

    environmental education and community development (ICENECDEV) and Sustainable Run for Development (SURUDEV).

    He is a member of Junior Chamber International (JCI) and has served as the Executive Vice-President (JCI Buea Summit),

    Commonwealth Youth Peace Ambassadors Network (CYPAN), Youth Alliance for Leadership and Development in Africa (YALDA) and

    Young African Leaders Initiative Network (YALI NETWORK).
    He has organized and train youth on peace and democracy, civic education, climate change amongst others.

    He was selected among 90 world peace activists of the year 2017 by PEACEMAKERS360 in Columbia.

    He is a board member of the Multipurpose Youth Empowerment Center Buea (MYEC).
    Samuel has had audience with the commonwealth secretary general Rt.Hon. Patricia Scotland, UK Prime

    Minister invitee at the Heads of Government and foreign ministers reception sky garden London.

    He has attended several conferences, Seminars, trainings and workshops on peace, leadership,

    community development, environment, democracy, and security amongst others.

    Samuel has received certificate of Honors for Peace from the iBUILDAFRICA Peace Center in Morocco,

    two international global peace awards, Commonwealth trainer of trainers, Fellow of the International

    Governance Institute regional fellowship on Conciliatory Peace making and Democratic Governance,

    Alumni of the REPAIR SERIES Peace and Democracy Program, Fellow of the Emerging Leaders in Cameroon

    cohort one on Policy and Public service by Denis and Lenora Foretia Foundation, “Books for Peace” Award

    Laureate (Rome/Italy), youth delegate to UN ECOSOC.
    He is a Certified Trainer on Effective Non-violent Mobilization, in Accra - Ghana by West Africa Civil

    Society Institute (WACSI) in collaboration with OSIWA.

    2019 Cameroon National Achievement Award Recognition as National Outstanding Peace Ambassador in Cameroon.

    2019 Cameroon Heroes Award Winner as Most Impact Peace Advocate of the Year and Certified Commonwealth Peace

    Trainer of Trainers
    He is a Global peace Ambassador, Global Youth Ambassador, Global Goodwill Ambassador, Global Active Citizen,

    United People Global Champion and Ambassador of Fotabe University in Cameroon.

    Consulted with the Commonwealth secretariat on the Cameroon peace process, consulted with the United

    Nations International Organization for Migration (UN-IOM) on baseline assessment on community needs during

    crisis situation in the southwest region of Cameroon, consulted with German Cooperation (GIZ) on community

    access to civil status registry facilities in the southwest and littoral region of Cameroon.
    Samuel through his consulting firm “GSOLUT Consulting” is helping organizations, institutions, corporations

    and companies understand the role of peace building at the workplace for staff peak performance,

    increased productivity and sustainable relevance.

    He is also through his youth focused academy “Peace Academy” empowering emerging leaders with knowledge,

    skills and tools to thrive in governance and to impact their world as trained change agents and certified peace advocates.
